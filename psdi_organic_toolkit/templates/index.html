PSDI Variability Plot Generator

Web tool to produce plots that describe the reproducibility of a numerical reaction outcome. The output scatterplots display the mean value of the outcome and its associated confidence interval.

PSDI Glorius Plot Generator

Web tool to visualise the sensitivity of a given chemical transformation to user-defined reaction conditions. The output plots are closely based on an original concept from the Glorius research group.
